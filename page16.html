unit 1, outcome 2:

2.4 Describe further improvements you can make to your project


I could improve my project as a whole by working to my full potential and produce something that warrants being called a designer. I think maybe the layout of the website could have more complicated but concise elements to it, making it more dynamic for the user experience. I could improve the quality and use of imagery. Blurry images, or an overreliance on joyless stock images, make a website seem amateurish. I could make the copy text to-the-point and clear, copy text should point users to an action without overdoing it.
